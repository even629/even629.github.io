Problem:

P1143 Longest Common Subsequence

https://leetcode.cn/problems/longest-common-subsequence/description/?envType=study-plan-v2&envId=top-100-liked

It’s a classic problem,dp[i][j]denotestext1[0..=i]andtext2[0..=j]the length of the longest common subsequence, the state transition equation is

  • When text1[i]==text2[j] text1[i] == text2[j] text1[i]==text2[j]:
    • dp[i][j]=dp[i−1][j−1]+1 dp[i][j] = dp[i-1][j-1] + 1 dp[i][j]=dp[i−1][j−1]+1
  • otherwise:
    • dp[i][j]=max(dp[i−1][j],dp[i][j−1]) dp[i][j] = max(dp[i-1][j], dp[i][j-1]) dp[i][j]=max(dp[i−1][j],dp[i][j−1])
