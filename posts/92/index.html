Problem:

P92 Reverse Linked List II

https://leetcode.cn/problems/reverse-linked-list-ii/description/?envType=study-plan-v2&envId=top-interview-150

In linked list problems, you generally cannot directly change the value of nodes.

Using the head insertion method to reverse a linked list, creating a dummy head node can avoid some special cases. Also, at the end, you should not return head, but virt_head->next, because head may change due to swapping.
