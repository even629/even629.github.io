Cell merging (merge cells)

Through themarkdown-it-merge-cells-plusPlugin merges cells on demand: entering a marker in a cell triggers merging; by default, adjacent identical cells are not merged.

  • <- Merge current cell to theleftcell (horizontal /colspan）
  • ^- Merge current cell to thetopcell (vertical /rowspan）

Header row can only use<left-right merging, not top-bottom merging;^Only effective from the second body row (the first body row is adjacent to the header and cannot be merged into the header). The merged area must form a rectangle.
