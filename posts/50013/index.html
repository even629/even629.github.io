Merge cells

Throughmarkdown-it-merge-cells-plusPlugin merges cells on demand: filling in a marker in a cell triggers merging; by default, adjacent identical cells are not merged.

  • <- Merge current cell toLeftCell (horizontal /colspan）
  • ^- Merge current cell toAboveCell (vertical /rowspan）

Header row can only use<Merge left and right, cannot merge up and down;^Only effective from the second row of body text (the first row of body text is adjacent to the header and cannot be merged into the header). The merged area must form a rectangle.
