P3186 Maximum Total Damage of Spells

https://leetcode.cn/problems/maximum-total-damage-with-spell-casting/description/?envType=daily-question&envId=2025-10-11

Since there may be spells with the same damage, and if multiple spells with the same damage are selected, the total damage should add that damage * the number of spells with that damage. Therefore, we can first save the number of spells for each damage value, and then deduplicate the power.
After deduplicating and sorting the power, let f(i) represent the maximum total damage when selecting from the 0th to the i-th type of spell, and the last selected spell is the i-th type. The state transition equation can be listed as:
